FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

Please order as early as possible, especially for birthdays, graduations, and holidays.

Can I customize colors and themes?

Yes. Colors, themes, names, numbers, and design ideas can be added in the order form.

Are prices final?

Prices start at the listed amount and may increase depending on detail level and custom requests.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. A 50% deposit is required for custom orders before the order is confirmed.

Do you offer pickup or delivery?

Pickup and delivery options can be requested in the order form. Final details will be confirmed after review.

Can I send inspiration photos?

Yes. Mention your ideas in the order form, then DM inspiration photos to @dippedbykohen.
